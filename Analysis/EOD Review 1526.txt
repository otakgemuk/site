EOD Review 01/05/26

Market started gapping up from the EMA from Pre-Market since Bar 61-Pre
A strong indication of a continuation higher as price closes above the EMA
Market moves sideways since bar 61Pre which also serves as the Motherbar
Using a Fib Retracement to do a BLSH would have worked gives 29 ticks one way

Bar 1 was a Breakout Bar breaking the range from Bar 61 (Pre) to Bar 76 (Pre)
Placing a Buy STOP 1 tick above Bar 61 expecting range breakout gives high probability of win

T01
Entry Buy STOP 1 tick above Bar 61 (Pre) and STOP below Bar 68 lowest point of the range
Using the Range from Pre-Market as MM up taking high of Bar 64 (Pre) as exhaustion point
Exit at Bar 7 for a 1:2 RR gives 101 ticks

Bar 5 to Bar 7 is Leg 1 and Bar 9 to Bar 11 is Leg 2
Bar 8/9 is Low 1 and Bar 10/11 is Low 2
After spike move up, market tends to reverse after 2 legs and L2 is a good Entry for Short

T02
Entry Sell STOP 1 tick Below Bar 10 after Bar 12 close at its low
Using Bar 10/11 as MM down expecting 1:2.5 RR move
Exit at Bar 14 for 1:2.5 RR gives 71 ticks

Markets move 2 legs down as expected Bar 12 to Bar 14 as Leg 1 and Bar 17 to Bar 19 as Leg 2
Bar 15/16 is a High 1 and Bar 20/21 is High 2
After 2 legs down, high probability of markets reverses direction
while Price closes above the EMA at Bar 21 - at H2 signal

T03
Entry Buy STOP 1 tick above Bar 21 and STOP 1 tick below Bar 20
Using Bar 20/21 as MM up expecting another 2 legs up
Exit at Bar 30 gives 27 ticks
note: The target as 1:2.5 however Bar 29 reached price exhaustion signal as TD9
Price did not hit the STOP during the Bar 29 pullback and price reverses back up 
and move exit to 1:1 RR filled at Bar 30.
The trade was invalidated for the expected target, and managed to secure profit 
on the pullback.

Sticking to the process is very important although in reality after 1:1 target is reached
STOP should be moved while aiming for 1:2.5 RR target.
In this case, I did move the stop as market was performing as expected until Bar 29 
noise move.

After the noise, it was not worthed to continue trading as market becomes very 
difficult as surprising headlines continue to enter the market and its Friday.
It was best to walk away after securing profits.
and on top of that CVD starts to move lower after the noise.
