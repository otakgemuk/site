Market was in a trading range behavior until a breakout occurs, making it a limit order market. Bar 8/9 formed a High 1 and Bar 13/14 formed an H2 — a good indication of markets moving higher. Bar 15 closes at its high and above the close of Bar 1, which some consider a breakout point.

T01 Execution Context

Entry was a Buy 1 tick above Bar 14, filled at Bar 16. Using Bar 13/14 for a Measured Move up. Exit at Bar 28 — 1:7 R:R, +30 points. From Bar 28 onwards, bars started overlapping again — an indication of market moving sideways, a trading range market behavior. Bar 28 can be considered a Mother Bar encapsulating the bars moving up and down, so it was good to walk away with 30 points secured.

T02–T05: MFR Oscillation

Four subsequent trades were taken using Modified Fib Retracement (MFR) levels at .21 and .79, alternating buy and sell limit orders within the range. Each captured 9 points. No trades got filled at .21 MFR after Bar 59 — a good indication that TR behavior either paused or price was moving higher. Best to walk away after 4 good trades.
