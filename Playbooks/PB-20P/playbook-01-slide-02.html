✓ Trend Day Signals
Gap open > 10 pts — holds direction in first 15 min
Price stays one side of VWAP for > 30 min
VIX > 18 with clear directional momentum
High-impact news catalyst confirms direction (FOMC, CPI, NFP)
Globex range > 30 pts overnight
Strategy: Buy every pullback. Never fade. Exit at measured move or EOD.
↔ Range Day Signals
Flat open — price within 5 pts of prior close
Price oscillates back and forth through VWAP repeatedly
VIX < 14 — low volatility, compressed ranges
No major catalysts on the economic calendar
Globex range < 15 pts — quiet overnight session
Strategy: Fade extremes — buy bottom third, sell top third of range. Scale out at midpoint.
⚠ No-Trade Zones: 30 min before/after FOMC, CPI, NFP releases · Opening range > 40 pts (wait for structure) · VIX spikes above 35 (reduce size 50% or sit out) · Already hit daily loss limit
