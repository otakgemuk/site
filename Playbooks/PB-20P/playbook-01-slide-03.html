Long Entries
VWAP Reclaim ↑
Price dips below VWAP then closes back above it on 5-min candle with volume confirmation.
HVN Bounce ↑
Rejection wick at High Volume Node from below — bar closes above the HVN level.
Breakout Confirm ↑
Break above prior high or Globex high — 5-min close above with a volume surge.
Opening Drive ↑
First 5-min candle closes bullish, retest holds — enter on break of that candle's high.
Short Entries
VWAP Rejection ↓
Price fails to close above VWAP from below — closes back under with volume confirming sellers.
LVN Fade ↓
Low Volume Node acts as resistance — short the rejection wick close at the LVN level.
Failed Breakout ↓
Price breaks a key level but closes back inside the range within 1–2 candles. Bull trap.
Opening Drive ↓
First 5-min candle closes bearish, rally back fails — short the break of that candle's low.
Exit Rules — Quick Reference
Take Profit: Exit 50% at first target (HVN or VWAP ±5–6 pts). Exit 50% at second target (+10 pts). Close all if price stalls 3+ candles.
Cut Loss: Exit if price closes back through entry trigger, VWAP turns against trade, or counter-trend volume spike appears. NEVER add to a loser.
