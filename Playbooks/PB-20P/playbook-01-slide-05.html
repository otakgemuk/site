8:30–9:30 AM
Pre-Market Prep
Mark key levels (Yesterday H/L · Globex H/L · VWAP · POC/HVN/LVN). Assess bias: Trend or Range day. Check economic calendar. Set daily loss limit & kill switch.
No trades — prep only
9:30–10:30 AM
Opening Drive
Highest volatility window. Breakout or fade the opening range. Momentum entries only — no counter-trend in first 15 min. Max 2 trades in this window.
Target: 5–8 pts
11:00 AM–1:30 PM
Midday Rotation
Play VWAP bounces and volume profile rotations. Smaller size, tighter stops (3–5 pts). Take partials quickly — midday moves are shorter and less clean.
Target: 5–7 pts
2:00–3:30 PM
Afternoon Push
Align with trend continuation or reversal into close. Use Fed / earnings / news catalysts if present. Complete the 20-point target. Scale: stagger exits at 10 pts + 20 pts.
Target: 6–8 pts
After 3:30 PM: Close all positions. No new trades. Log the day, review scores, set up for tomorrow.
Phases 1 & 2 Detail
Pre-Market Checklist: Yesterday H/L · Overnight range · Prior day VWAP · Major volume profile nodes (POC, HVN, LVN) · Bias assessment
Opening Breakout: Enter with momentum. Target 6–8 pts with partial exit at +5. Trail remainder.
Opening Fade: Fade extremes back to VWAP. Target 5–6 pts. Tighter stops at opening range extreme.
Risk Floor: Cap losses at 5–10 pts ($250–$500/ES contract). If stopped → reset, don't chase.
Phases 3, 4 & EOD
Midday: Harvest 5–7 pts. Play VWAP bounces. Smaller size, tighter stops. Partial exits: lock 5 pts, trail remainder.
Afternoon: Final 6–8 pts. Align with trend continuation. Use catalysts if present. Complete the 20-pt target.
Consistency Rule: Total harvest — 3 trades × 6–7 pts = 20 pts. Even 2 winning trades = net positive day.
EOD Review: Log all trades, score execution quality, note one improvement for tomorrow.
