Trend Day Entry
Wait for the breakout: Don't enter before the bracket is broken. Wait for a 5-min close above the bracket high.
Wait for the pullback: Don't chase the breakout. Let price pull back to the bracket high or VWAP.
Confirm the bounce: Wait for a bullish candle to form at the pullback level. Enter on the close of that candle.
Max 2 trades: If the first two trend entries fail, the day may not be a trend day. Reassess.
Range Day Entry
Wait for the touch: Price must touch the bracket edge. Don't enter in the middle of the range.
Wait for the rejection: A wick through the bracket edge followed by a close back inside = rejection.
Enter on confirmation: Fishing signal fires (stop-run + close back inside). Enter at close.
Target the opposite edge: Take profit at the other bracket edge. Scale out at VWAP.
⚠ No Entry If: EMA bias opposes the trade direction · Day type is unclear · Price is 20+ points from VWAP · News release within 15 minutes · Already hit daily loss limit · Stop is wider than 10 points
